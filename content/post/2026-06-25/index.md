I successfully received a [Geographic Information Systems Professional](https://www.gisci.org/Want-to-be-a-GISP/Become-a-GISP) (GISP) certification from the [Geographic Information Systems Certification Institute](https://www.gisci.org/) (GISCI). 

<!--more-->

The GISCI program is an examination-based, world-recognized certification, that includes an exam and self-documented and peer reviewed application as the evaluation mechanism. This program is for established GIS professionals interested in earning recognition from a credible 3rd party certification institute. It is a way of deepening one's commitment to the field of GIS and for building professional esteem.

As of June 2026, I demonstrated that I have:
- at least 4 years of professional experience
- met the requirements of the Portfolio Review process, and
- passed the GISCI Core Technical Exam®
