I hosted [Dr. David Wheeler](https://medschool.vcu.edu/expertise/detail.html?id=dcwheeler), Associate Professor in the Department of Biostatistics within the School of Medicine at [Virginia Commonwealth University](https://www.vcu.edu) (VCU), for the [National Cancer Institute](https://www.cancer.gov/) [Cancer Prevention Fellowship Program](https://cpfp.cancer.gov/) [Cancer Prevention and Control Colloquia Series](https://cpfp.cancer.gov/colloquia). 

<!--more-->

He gave a talk entitled, "Estimating Neighborhood Disadvantage Indices for Health Outcomes." I moderated the hour-long virtual session.

**Update** - 2024-06-29: Sadly, [Dr. Wheeler passed away in 2024](https://biostatistics.vcu.edu/people/faculty/David%20Wheeler) after a hard-fought battle with brain cancer.

**Update** - 2025-04-04: He is honored by the VCU [Department of Biostatistics](https://biostatistics.vcu.edu) in the [School of Public Health](https://sph.vcu.edu/) with its [Dr. David Wheeler Memorial Lecture in Spatial and Cancer Statistics](https://www.masseycancercenter.org/news/memorial-lecture-series-at-vcu-honors-david-wheeler/).
