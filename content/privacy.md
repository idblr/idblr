This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you visit this website.

### 1. Information Collected Automatically
When you visit this site, certain information about your device is automatically collected:
*   **Hosting Logs:** This website is hosted by [Netlify](https://www.netlify.com/privacy/). Netlify automatically collects access logs, which include your IP address, browser type, and time of visit, to provide and maintain their hosting services.
*   **Analytics:** We use [Google Analytics](https://developers.google.com/analytics) to understand how visitors interact with the site. We have configured Google Analytics to **anonymize your IP address**. Google Analytics 4 (GA4) does not store individual IP addresses by default.

### 2. Cookies
We may use "cookies" to enhance your experience. 
*   **Google Analytics Cookies:** These help us track site usage and performance. You can opt out of these by using the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).
*   **Academic Theme:** This site uses the [Hugo Blox (formerly Academic) theme](https://github.com/HugoBlox), which may include a cookie consent banner to inform you of these practices.

### 3. Third-Party Services
This site is a static website generated with [Hugo](https://gohugo.io) and [blogdown](https://github.com/rstudio/blogdown). It does not have a database and does not directly collect personal information through forms or user accounts unless you explicitly provide it (e.g., via an email link).

### 4. Your Rights
Depending on your location (such as the EEA), you may have rights under the **GDPR** or other data protection laws, including the right to access or delete any personal data held about you. 

### 5. Contact
If you have questions about this policy, please [contact me](/profile).
